ALISHER NAVOI PARK


Tashkent’s Alisher Navoi National Park is one of the greatest city parks in the country. Covering a large park, a large lake, canals and fountains, a meadow and ornamental flower gardens, there’s a good chance you’ll forget you are right in the center of Tashkent. Alisher Navoi National Park is the green heart of the city and it is the best place to escape the city life. People run, swim, climb, ride or simple sit and read books in huge parks different scenery. although the park looks like a natural, its man made. National Park in Tashkent has something for everyone. You can enjoy boating, cycling and skating. There are some amusement park rides and a large manmade lake, where you can find people swimming in the warm months. The Park has lakeside restaurants and cafes which serve everything from a three-course meal to a quick cup of coffee. National Park is home to a number of fascinating buildings and monuments, such as the Oliy Majlis (parliament), the Wedding Palace, medieval Abulkasym madrassah, Istiqlol Concert Hall and the famous monument to Alisher Navoi. The National holidays and open air events are celebrated in the Park throughout the year, there’s always something to see and do in the National park in Tashkent.
